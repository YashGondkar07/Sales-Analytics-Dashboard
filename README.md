## 🚀 Project Overview

The **Sales Analytics Dashboard** is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segments, and regional trends.

This dashboard helps businesses make data-driven decisions by providing insights into revenue growth, profit margins, product performance, and sales distribution.

---

## 🎯 Project Objectives

* Analyze overall sales performance
* Track monthly sales trends
* Identify top-performing products
* Compare regional sales and profits
* Monitor customer segments
* Create an interactive business dashboard

## 🔍 Key Insights

* West region generated the highest sales revenue.
* Technology category contributed the largest share of sales.
* Top-performing products accounted for a significant portion of total revenue.
* Monthly sales trends revealed seasonal fluctuations.
* Profitability varied across different regions and customer segments.
